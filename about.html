About my profile

A cybersecurity enthusiast with a strong commitment to continuous learning and staying abreast of the latest technological advancements. Dedicated to addressing evolving cyber threats and leveraging expertise to enhance digital security through proactive strategies and innovative solutions. I have achieved both the ISC² Certified in Cybersecurity (CC) and CompTIA Security+ (SY0-701) certifications, demonstrating proficiency in cybersecurity principles, risk management, governance, and core security functions.
I have gained valuable hands-on experience through internships at Tsaaro Academy, Ciaro, and Schneider Electric. At Tsaaro Academy, I focused on data privacy and protection, honing essential skills in safeguarding sensitive information and mitigating potential risks. These experiences have not only expanded my technical knowledge but also honed my problem-solving abilities and communication skills in a professional environment. I am now well-equipped to contribute effectively to safeguard digital landscapes and mitigate cyber threats with a proactive and innovative approach.
